## AI is expensive, so why is everyone suddenly trying to use it for all sorts of automation?

If you're following AI at all you know that the hype is palpable online and in the media these days. And with it that magical quality that makes it seem like it's going to solve all the problems, at the click of a button, or a prompt in this case.

The problem is that for all it's incredible capabilities AI isn't good at everything. There's a time and a place to use it. This is a lesson that those of us who've ever paid a bill for API tokens have already begun to learn. Depending on the complexity of the task and the model, you could quickly end up spending hundreds, or even thousands of dollars on tokens.

The good news is that it doesn't have to be this way. Learning when to employ AI is at least as important as knowing how. In this article, I'm going to get into this by breaking down the use cases where AI is, and is not helpful, and discuss some tips and tricks you can use to get the most out of your monthly usage.
